About Collective Solutions

This is an online tool that helps match people with problems that need to be solved.

How it works:

  1. Create a profile, including your skills and any personal qualities that might be relevant
  2. Browse the problems that need to be solved
  3. Check if there are tasks to do that you know you can help solve
  4. Bid to contribute to a specific problem
  5. Meet your team, learn your domain, and get to work!
  6. Optionally, contribute to problems by submitting ideas or upvoting your favorites
