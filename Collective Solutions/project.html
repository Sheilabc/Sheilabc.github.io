The vision

Humans rely on the ocean for it's beauty and for the marine life that it supports. Plastics are making it difficult for marine wildlife to live in the oceans. If we don't remove the plastic, many animals might die and we will never get them back.

Goals

  • Reduce the amount of plastic in the oceans by 2% each year, starting in 2023
  • Develop new technologies for removing plastics from the ocean
  • Coordinate with world governments to sign legislation that bans dumping plastic in the oceans
  • Run a public awareness campaign to reduce usage of plastics
  • Research alternatives to plastic that can be used for consumer goods

Non-goals

  • Move plastic from the ocean directly into nearby landfills
